How do wireframes play a role in RWD?

Wireframes play a huge role in RWD because they help the designers sketch out their ideas and figure out how their design layouts are going to work for their sites.

What is responsive web design?

Responsive Web Design is about using CSS and HTML to resize, hide, shrink, enlarge, or move the content to make it look good on any screen.
